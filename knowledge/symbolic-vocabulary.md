# Symbolic Vocabulary

Use this document for broader symbolic associations that can enrich readings beyond the per-card lore.

Stub notes to expand:

- Clubs / Wands: energy, initiative, will, desire, work in motion.
- Hearts / Cups: feeling, relationship, receptivity, memory, belonging.
- Spades / Swords: thought, language, conflict, clarity, decisions.
- Diamonds / Pentacles: body, money, craft, resources, material life.
- Aces often introduce raw potential.
- Tens often show culmination, saturation, or the burden of completion.
